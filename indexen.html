About the HUB

The Design Incubation HUB is an ecosystem that connects academic and productive units related to the Industrial Design degree and the Design Lab (LAD).

We act as a collaborative community focused on transforming ideas into creative and strategic ventures. We support students developing their final degree projects (TIFs) and pre-professional practices (PPPs), as well as entrepreneurs, cooperatives, and small and medium-sized enterprises.

Our goal is to offer incubation and technical assistance for the development of design projects, promoting innovation and sustainable growth.

We have a multidisciplinary team of professionals providing advice on design, business management, and marketing, along with access to resources and tools to enhance project success.

The HUB seeks to foster creativity, collaboration, and continuous learning, generating a positive impact in the entrepreneurial ecosystem and contributing to regional economic and social development.

We provide tools for diagnosis, design, training, coaching in management and finance, and development focused on innovation. We believe in collaboration, purpose, and comprehensive development as engines of change.
